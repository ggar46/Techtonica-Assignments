Pan De Elote

It's been a long time since my mom has made corn bread, but I remember how nice it smelled and how my sister and I would argue over who could finish the lechera. Ironically, I'm not a fan of corn, so this is the only way I'll enjoy it. Like many other mothers, my mom does not specify all quantities, so feel free to adjust to your own taste.
