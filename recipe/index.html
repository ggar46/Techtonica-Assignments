Pan De Elote

It's been a long time since my mom has made corn bread, but I remember how nice it smelled and how my sister and I would argue over who could finish the lechera. Ironically, I'm not a fan of corn, so this is the only way I'll enjoy it. Like many other mothers, my mom does not specify all quantities, so feel free to adjust to your own taste.;

Picture Credit: Recetas Gratis

Ingredients

  • 8 fresh ears of corn
  • 1 can of Lechera
  • sugar (to taste)
  • 8 eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups of flour
  • 2 tablespoons baking powder (Royal)
  • Vanilla
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 stick of butter

Instructions

  1. "Grind" the corn with the eggs and a bit of vanilla and sugar.
  2. Beat the mixture with the flour and milk.
  3. Prepare the baking container for the oven with butter and flour.
  4. Right before adding the mixture into the oven, mix in the Royal.
